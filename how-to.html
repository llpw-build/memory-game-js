How to play

  • Click on any two cards to flip them over
  • Try to find matching pairs of the cards
  • You score 3 points for each pair
  • If the cards do not match, they will turn back over
  • You have just 30 seconds to find all of the pairs
  • The timer will start when you flip the first card
  • Fewer moves and more time left when finding all of the pairs will give you a higher ranking
  • When you complete the game, your score will be added to the high score page
  • You can restart the game whenever you would like with the restart button
